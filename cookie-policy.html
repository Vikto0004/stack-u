Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. By continuing to use this site, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. These files help us provide a more personalized and efficient service. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your data.

Cookies are an essential tool for modern websites as they make navigation more convenient and efficient. By continuing to use our site, you confirm your agreement to the use of cookies as described in this policy. If you wish to refuse the use of cookies, you can configure your browser settings accordingly.

1. What is a Cookie?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences for a certain period, so you don’t have to re-enter this information each time you visit. Cookies can be set by the site itself (first-party cookies) or by third parties (e.g., advertising platforms).

Cookies typically contain the following information: the name of the server that created them, their lifespan, and a unique identifier. Some cookies are necessary for the site to function, while others help track user behavior for marketing and analytics purposes. There are temporary cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (stored on your device for a defined period or until manually deleted).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies to enhance your experience on the site. They serve various functions: ensuring the site operates correctly, personalizing content, and analyzing user behavior.

  • Necessary Cookies: Ensure the proper functioning of the site. They enable navigation and access to secure sections.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how you use the site (e.g., the pages you visit most often) to improve the site's functionality and performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language, location, and login information, to make the site more convenient to use.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Social Media Cookies: Allow content sharing on social media and track interactions with that content.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help collect data on site visits, traffic sources, and user behavior so we can improve the site's structure and content.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Ensure the proper functioning and improve the performance of the site.
  • Remember your preferences and login information.
  • Personalize content and offers.
  • Analyze user interactions with the site for optimization.
  • Display targeted ads based on your interests.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can modify these settings to block cookies or receive notifications about their use. Disabling cookies may limit access to certain site features.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We collaborate with trusted partners, such as advertising networks, analytics platforms, and social media networks, which may use cookies to provide personalized content. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

6. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to modify this cookie policy. All updates will be posted on this page with the date of the last modification. We recommend regularly reviewing this policy to stay informed about changes.

In case of significant changes, we will notify you via a message on the site or by email if you have provided your contact details.
