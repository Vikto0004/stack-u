Our History

How It All Began

StackU was founded in 2010 in Toronto with a clear vision: to revolutionize the financial education sector in Canada. From the beginning, our goal was to provide high-quality financial services based on innovation and client satisfaction.

Over the years, we have expanded our expertise and service portfolio to meet the evolving needs of our clients. Through our commitment to excellence, we have become a trusted name in financial education in Canada.

Today, StackU continues to grow and evolve while staying true to its original mission: helping our clients achieve their financial goals successfully.
