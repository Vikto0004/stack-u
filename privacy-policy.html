Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when using our site. We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws.

By using our site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions or comments, please contact us using the details below.

1. Data Collected

We collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other data you provide through forms on the site.
  • Usage Data: Your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and duration of visits.
  • Cookies: To enhance your experience on the site (see our Cookie Policy).
  • Advertising Data: We use Google Ads to collect information about your interactions with our advertisements. This data may include information about clicks, impressions, and conversions.

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Personalize your user experience.
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests.
  • Send marketing messages, if you have consented.
  • Analyze site usage to make future improvements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service providers who help us manage the site or deliver our services.
  • Public authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing.
  • File a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

6. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. All changes will be posted on this page with the date of the last update. We recommend regularly reviewing this policy to stay informed about changes.
